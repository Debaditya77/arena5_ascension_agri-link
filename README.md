🌾 AgriLink: The AI Co-Pilot for Farmers

🚀 Overview

AgriLink is a hyperlocal, AI-powered platform designed to reduce agricultural waste and increase farmer income by intelligently connecting farmers with nearby buyers for surplus produce.

Unlike traditional marketplaces such as eNAM or Ninjacart, AgriLink focuses specifically on urgent surplus management, enabling farmers to sell excess crops quickly before spoilage.

⸻

❗ Problem Statement

A significant portion of harvested crops in India goes to waste due to:
	•	Lack of immediate buyers
	•	Poor price discovery
	•	Inefficient logistics
	•	Limited access to technology

Farmers often incur losses despite having valuable produce.

⸻

💡 Our Solution

AgriLink acts as an AI Co-Pilot that assists farmers in:
	•	Identifying surplus crops
	•	Finding nearby buyers instantly
	•	Getting fair price insights
	•	Managing sales through a simple, intuitive interface

⸻

✨ Key Features

🧠 AI-Powered Intelligence
	•	Crop recognition via image scanning
	•	AI-based surplus and pricing insights
	•	Real-time demand awareness

🎤 Voice-First Interaction
	•	Large mic-based interface for zero-typing usage
	•	Designed for accessibility and low digital literacy

📍 Hyperlocal Buyer Matching
	•	Connects farmers to nearby buyers (shops, restaurants, NGOs)
	•	Distance-based prioritization for faster transactions

📊 Smart Dashboard
	•	Live crop price trends
	•	Weather alerts
	•	Cold storage availability

🤝 Direct Buyer Marketplace
	•	Clean listing of verified buyers
	•	Transparent pricing and distance display
	•	Swipe-to-accept interaction for quick deals

🚨 Urgency-Based Selling
	•	Highlights time-sensitive surplus crops
	•	Helps prevent spoilage and waste

🎨 Design Philosophy

AgriLink follows a modern, mobile-first design system:
	•	🌑 Dark Mode Dominance for reduced strain and clarity
	•	🧊 Glassmorphism UI for clean, layered visuals
	•	🟩 Neon green accents for agricultural identity
	•	📦 Bento grid layout for structured information flow

The interface is designed to be:

Minimal • Accessible • Data-driven • Intuitive
